Pigeon Forge

The Old Mill Pottery House Cafe & Grille

Award-winning Southern dining at the heart of Old Mill Square. Breads are baked fresh daily from grain milled on-site — the sourdough and onion-rye are standouts. Named 2022 Best Family Restaurant in Pigeon Forge. Lunch and dinner daily; weekend brunch Saturday and Sunday starting at 10 a.m.

Lunch & Dinner Weekend Brunch 2022 Best Family Restaurant

Seymour

Seymour Grille

A dependable neighborhood restaurant on Chapman Highway in Seymour, open daily from 6 a.m. through evening — one of the earlier starts in the Smokies corridor. Online ordering available and full catering for groups. A practical stop whether you're heading into the mountains or heading home.

Open Daily 6 AM–9 PM Online Ordering Catering Available

Sevierville

Tony Gore's Smoky Mountain BBQ & Grill

Slow-smoked barbecue meets live music on Winfield Dunn Parkway in Sevierville. Owner Tony Gore is a performing musician who regularly takes the stage alongside lunch and dinner service. Open Thursday through Saturday until 8 p.m.; Sunday through Tuesday until 4 p.m. Closed Wednesdays.
